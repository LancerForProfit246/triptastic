About Triptastic

Established in 2022, Triptastic Nursing and Employment Agency is a trusted provider of professional staffing solutions and healthcare services in Barbados and beyond.

We connect qualified nurses, caregivers, and skilled professionals with hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, private clients, and businesses across various sectors. Our focus on excellence, compassion, and personalized support ensures the right people are matched with the right opportunities every time.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional staffing and healthcare solutions that promote quality care, professional growth, and community well-being. We connect compassionate, skilled professionals with reputable employers, ensuring reliable service delivery and lasting partnerships built on trust, integrity, and excellence.

Our Services

Comprehensive staffing and care solutions tailored to your needs

Nursing & Healthcare Staffing

Registered Nurses (RNs), Licensed Practical Nurses, Nursing Assistants, Home Health Aides, and Personal Support Workers for hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, and private care.

Private Home Care Services

Compassionate in-home care including companionship, personal assistance, medication reminders, meal preparation, and specialized dementia/Alzheimer’s support.

General Employment Services
